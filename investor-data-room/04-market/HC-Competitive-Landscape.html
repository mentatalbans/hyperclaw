Our Six Unfair Advantages
Routing

UCB1 HyperRouter — Self-Improving

HyperRouter uses Upper Confidence Bound (UCB1) bandit algorithms to learn optimal model-to-task assignment. Gets smarter with every interaction. Formula: ucb1_score = mean_reward + C × √(ln(total) / attempts). No competitor has this.

Memory

Three-Tier Civilizational Memory

Session, Domain, and Civilizational Graph (pgvector) — permanent, growing memory. Causal relationships preserved across tiers. LangChain's buffer and CrewAI's lack of persistent memory are no match.

Safety

HyperShield + Three Human Gates

Zero-trust, hot-reload YAML policies. Three Human Gates baked in: Ethical, Authorization, and Uncertainty (<60% confidence escalates to human review). No competitor ships a dedicated policy engine.

Agents

50+ Specialist Agents OOTB

Across 5 domains, HyperClaw ships production-ready specialist agents managed by SOLOMON overmind. Competitors ship templates and examples — customers must build everything themselves.

Self-Expansion

GENESIS Protocol — Unique in Market

SOLOMON automatically detects capability gaps and creates new specialist agents via the GENESIS Protocol. The agent network grows with the organization. No other platform has self-expanding agents — period.

Knowledge

Civilization Knowledge Base

Ingests SOPs, org charts, job descriptions, client profiles, runbooks, and policies. Procedural chunker preserves step order. Interview agent extracts tacit knowledge. OrgGraph traversal provides role-aware context injection to every agent.
